### Visualisation(s)

The code here shows how to reproduce, in part, Figure 7 of [Batson _et al_. (2021)](https://elifesciences.org/articles/68353) and Figure 1A of [Dudas & Batson (2023)](https://journals.asm.org/doi/full/10.1128/jvi.01056-23), as well as other visualisations that others might find useful.
